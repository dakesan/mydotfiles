## Usage Guidelines

### In Meeting Notes

**Participants Section**:
```markdown
Lx: 大嶽, 岩崎, 簗島
External: Company representative names
```

**In Content**:
- Use Japanese surname only for brevity: `大嶽より説明した`
- Use full Japanese name when clarity needed: `相澤慎一先生との議論`
- Use English name in English context: `Odake explained...`

**DO NOT**:
- ❌ `[[OdakeHiro]]`
- ❌ `[[大嶽]]`
- ❌ `[[Aizawa]]`

**DO**:
- ✅ `大嶽より説明した`
- ✅ `相澤先生からのコメント`
- ✅ `Odake explained the strategy`

### External Participants

For external participants (vendors, partners, candidates), you may use wikilinks when appropriate:
- ✅ `[[CompanyName/PersonName]]` for external collaborators
- ✅ Company names as wikilinks: `[[Vertex Pharmaceuticals]]`

## Name Variations

### 相澤先生 (Aizawa-sensei)
- Common references: Yas, Sensei, 相澤先生
- Context: Research advisor, professor
- Use "先生" suffix to show respect

### Others
- Generally use surname only in Japanese context
- Full name in formal documents or when clarity needed
